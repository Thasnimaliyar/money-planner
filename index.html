🧠 Core Budgeting Frameworks

Before recording specific transactions, choose a foundational method to structure every dollar that enters your wallet:

The 50/30/20 Rule

Divide your allowance or income into three strict buckets:
• 50% Needs: Essential food, transport, and school materials.
• 30% Wants: Entertainment, hobbies, and eating out with friends.
• 20% Savings: Set aside instantly for future long-term goals.

 

The "Pay Yourself First" Method

The moment you receive pocket money, immediately route a fixed percentage (e.g., 25%) directly into an unreachable savings or investment account. You are entirely free to spend whatever remains without tracking micro-categories.
